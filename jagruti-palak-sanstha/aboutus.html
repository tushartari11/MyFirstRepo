About Jagruti Palak Sanstha

There are innumerable difficulties in bringing up and supporting a mentally challenged child (Also called PWIDD-Person with intellectual and developmental disability). Individually parents face insurmountable odds. Collectively; they can share the burden and hope to work out a solution. The parents of such children in Thane therefore decided to come together and form a trust, to pool their resources and energies to face the challenge. Thus Jagruti Palak Sanstha was formed.

Jagruti Palak Sanstha, Thane was registered in 2002 with the Charity Commissioner of Thane.  It is also registered with National Trust, New Delhi and Parivaar (National federation of parents` association).  It has about 350 parent members and they mostly belong to the lower economic and social strata of the society. Besides, the elected executive committee, the centre also has an advisory board, to provide necessary vision and guidance.

JAGRUTI regularly conducts workshops, seminars, medical camps and get together of parents, where information regarding legal guardianship, insurance schemes etc is given to the parents. Parents come together to jointly celebrate various events which fosters friendship and comradeship between them. Presently it is running the ‘Spoorthi Activity Centre’ and the ‘Mamata Day Care Centre’ for disabled persons above 18 years of age , who are no longer supported by the special schools. These centres go a long way in helping parents to go about their jobs and other personal works without  worrying . about their wards during the day. Bringing up a mentally deficient child is a big challenge for any parent, more so if he or she belongs to the financially weaker section of the society and/or are daily wage earners. (Some of them may even be single parents). Though these children grow up physically, they are not capable of taking care of themselves due to their mental handicap. This puts the parents to extreme stress leading to frustration and even suicide.

Our Mission

To establish a permanent residential complex which will provide a lifetime shelter for mentally challenged persons.
 

The parents grow old they are worried about the child’s future after their (parent’s) death. With this backdrop in mind the only viable option was to establish a permanent residential complex which will provide a lifetime shelter for such mentally challenged persons. It was also necessary that such a shelter should also attempt to provide to some extent, vocational development avenues to the PWIDD .Also parents should be part of the management staying on the campus so that the PWIDD  are under  their sensitive loving care. After lots of efforts by the management committee, `JAGRUTI` has succeeded in locating a place for its proposed project at Taliye Village, Taluka Patan, Dist Satara in Maharashtra.
